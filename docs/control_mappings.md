# Control Mappings

This document maps elements of the IGA lab to common governance and compliance expectations.

## Access Control

- RBAC implements least-privilege principles.
- Role and entitlement definitions support consistent provisioning.
- Lifecycle workflows ensure timely access changes.

## Certification and Attestation

- Templates mirror standard access review documentation.
- Evidence samples demonstrate auditable review outcomes.

## Audit Readiness

- Removal logs support traceability for deprovisioning.
- Workflows demonstrate accountability and control enforcement.

## Separation of Duties (SoD)

- Business role modeling supports the identification of conflicting access.
- Documentation outlines how SoD considerations fit into lifecycle reviews.
