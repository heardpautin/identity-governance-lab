# Audit Summary

This summary outlines the governance and audit considerations demonstrated in this lab.

## Access Control
- RBAC modeling supports least-privilege principles.
- Role definitions and entitlement mappings provide consistent access assignment.

## Lifecycle Governance
- Joiner, Mover, and Leaver workflows ensure timely and accurate access changes.
- All lifecycle updates are logged for traceability.

## Certifications
- Templates support both manager and application owner reviews.
- Evidence samples demonstrate how attestation data may be stored.

## Audit Readiness
- Documentation supports typical internal and external audit requirements.
- Artifacts illustrate how access decisions and changes can be reviewed and validated.
